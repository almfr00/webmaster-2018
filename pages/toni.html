Introducing AntonioIras

The prized jewel of Viktuuri & Co.

The AntonioIras AI Bot (named after Shakespearean Antonio from Merchant of Venice and Iras from Henry VIII) is a software program developed by Viktuuri & Co.’s founders for artistic impression, analysis, and aid services and functions. With each of the different versions of AntonioIras, clients from writers to programmers to business managers to web designers may utilize AntonioIras to its fullest extent.

As taken from surveys from over one hundred candidates, Viktuuri & Co.’s AntonioIras focuses on functionality and reliability for those both in the working and the recreational worlds.

Downloadable in the latest Version 5. 1. 4. As an installable executable or purchasable as the external connectible block, AntonioIras’s technology can accurately record the styles of different clients to recognize patterns, suggest edits, and reference outside information relating to the topic or work at hand to make an excellent partner in both the business and educations worlds, as well as the recreational world. This technology will work alongside its user to enhance the product of the user without alterations to the user’s style. Perfect for students, employees, and employers, AntonioIras is designed with every client in mind. Choose from any of three versions – with keys and multiple models included – to suit your personal needs.

Current models include : the language models (available in 2, 3, and 4 language packs) the writing composition model (equipped for prose, poetry, and non-fiction), the programming model (supports HTML and CSS, Visual Basic, Perl, and C++) the business model (advanced AI to oversee events, charts, trends, and make suggestions according to inputted data compared to outside information) the inclusive model (comes equipped with up to four models installed into one package). Keys are available either as student/teacher or employer/employee or recreational.

Capabilities

AntonioIras is an AI ware that is designed to function as a secretary to any work environment, be it school, public centers, work, or home. The development of AntonioIras is credited to leading software engineering company Viktuuri & Co. and its chief engineers : Will Shakespear and Alfred Hux. Thanks to their visionary advancements in artificial intelligence, VIktuuri & Co. is proud to present AntonioIras.

AntonioIras functions on the coding language of TSA – Technology and Service Alphabet – as a spin-off language based on a mixture of languages including OWL and C++ as designed by Viktuuri & Co. coding expert Gorge Oar. By functioning on TSA, AntonioIras is able to respond to search requests and execute tasks as given.

Logging is the primary feature of AntonioIras. According to keywords as inputted by the user to the AI figure, AntonioIras will routinely check statistics, figures, news stories, updates, and posts to the Internet in accordance to relevancy and organize them into viewing ‘packages’ that will appear on the navigation plane of the program’s window. The Database folder found under the program’s folder under Program Files (x86) is similarly organized, allowing for easy and quick organization in function as a working secretary.

The secondary features of AntonioIras are query response and suggestion. AntonioIras is designed with two methods of input : via oral communication and via text communication. The program listens for the voice of the user and tracks its speech to determine whether or not the AI is being addressed ; when the AI considers itself to be addressed by the user, it will respond with necessary information and suggestion. The text input is found directly below the AI’s figure on the AntonioIras window. Here, the AI will respond either verbally or textually to the query inputted. Outside of having response summoned by the user, the AI will follow user speech and input and respond according to its algorithms with suggestions, observances, and corrections. This feature may be turned off.
